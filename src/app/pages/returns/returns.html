Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchases and manufacturing orders. Learn about our clear, fair policies regarding returns, refunds, and reprints.

Customer Satisfaction Guarantee

inventory_2 1. Physical Products & Equipment

For standard physical items like 3D printers, filaments, resins, and accessories purchased from our catalog:

  • 30-Day Window: We accept returns within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • Condition: Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition that you received them.
  • Exceptions: Opened filament spools and resin bottles are strictly non-refundable due to moisture/UV exposure concerns, unless defective upon delivery.

precision_manufacturing 2. Custom 3D Printing Services

Because custom prints are explicitly manufactured to your specifications and uploaded 3D models, standard return policies do not apply.

Reprint & Refund Policy for Services:

  • Quality Issues: If a print arrives with significant visual defects, warping, or layer shifts that deviate from standard agreed tolerances, we will offer a free reprint or a full refund upon providing photographic evidence within 7 days of delivery.
  • Dimensional Inaccuracies: We guarantee dimensional accuracy within standard FDM/SLA industry tolerances (typically ±0.2mm). Inaccuracies beyond this will qualify for a reprint.
  • Model Errors: We are not responsible for refunds if the final print fails or is unusable due to inherent flaws in the customer-provided 3D model (e.g., non-manifold geometry, walls too thin, unrealistic overhangs without supports authorized).

local_shipping 3. Return Shipping

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

If you are shipping an item over $75, you should consider using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We don't guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

sync 4. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return or request a reprint for a custom order:

  1. Navigate to your Orders page.
  2. Select the specific order you wish to discuss.
  3. Click the "Contact Support" or "Report Issue" button associated with that order.
  4. Provide details and attach clear photographs if reporting a defect or quality issue.
