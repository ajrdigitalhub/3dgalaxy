schedule 1. Order Processing

Orders placed on 3DGalaxy's website are processed within [2] business days from the date of purchase. Please note that processing times may vary during peak seasons or due to unforeseen circumstances.

local_shipping 2. Shipping Methods

Shipping is free on all purchases above ₹3,500. For orders under ₹3,500, shipping is applicable according to the weight of the product.
